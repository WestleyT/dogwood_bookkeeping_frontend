Georgette Athanosios

Georgette has been working in the accounting field for over 40 years. She started her post-college career at ADP as an Account Executive, setting up payroll in the greater Philadelphia area.

She transitioned into the computer field as a consultant, teaching professionals software applications at companies such as AT&T, Merck Pharmaceutical, ETS, and Mercer County Community College.

In 2018 Georgette refocused on being a full-time bookkeeper, where she has since helped a wide variety of clients such as architecture firms, construction & maintenence contractors, and non-profit organizations, manage their finances.
